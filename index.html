About KM Gurukulam

At KM Gurukulam, we believe that every child’s early steps are the foundation for a lifetime of learning. Our school offers a warm, nurturing, and joyful environment where children grow with curiosity, creativity, and confidence.

Our Mission

To inspire a lifelong love of learning by creating a safe and stimulating atmosphere where children feel free to explore, engage, and express themselves.

  • Creative Arts
  • Music & Movement
  • Emotional & Social Growth

Meet Our Founder

Learn about the visionary behind KM Gurukulam — blending years of early education leadership with a deep passion for nurturing young minds.

Mrs. Nima Radhakrishnan, M.A., MTT, DYT

With over a decade of experience in early childhood education, our founder has always believed that nurturing a child's curiosity lays the foundation for lifelong learning. Her compassionate leadership, dedication to holistic education, and passion for young learners continue to shape KM Gurukulam into a home of joyful discovery.

As an educator and mentor, she fosters a loving and structured atmosphere where every child feels seen, heard, and encouraged to shine.
